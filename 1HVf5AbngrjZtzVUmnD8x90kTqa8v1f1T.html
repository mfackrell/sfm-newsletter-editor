Partners

We’re grateful for these partners and campaigns currently helping support Salty Farm Ministries:

  • The St. Joe Community Foundation, Inc. — Their support has enabled Salty Farm Ministries to provide access to showers, clean clothes, haircuts, and overall hope to those in need.
  • Shuckums PCB — This local business supports Salty Farm Ministries with resources that help meet needs and restore dignity within the community.
  • Destiny Worship Center - Panama City Beach — Partnering for their Spring Outreach mission to help stock the Giving Closet, serving hundreds of individuals every month.
  • Central Christian Church - Mt. Vernon and Central Christian Church - Carbondale — Faith partners who have helped restock the Giving Closet and freezer, making it possible to provide hot meals and clean clothes.
  • The Wicked Wheel — A corporate partner supporting Salty Farm Ministries as we serve our community.
  • Freedom House - Transitional Housing — Collaborating with Salty Farm Ministries to provide essential resources for individuals needing support.
  • Amazon Wishlist Donor (Cathy) — A generous donor who provided a significant amount of supplies including tents, sleeping bags, and hygiene items, directly impacting those on the streets.
