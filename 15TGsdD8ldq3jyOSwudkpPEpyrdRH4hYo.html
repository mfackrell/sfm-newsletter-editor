Volunteer Spotlight

To the Auburn University students, thanks for joining us in the streets tonight!!! It was an honor and privilege to serve alongside you.

They say "knowledge is power". I say "love is mighty." And this group is gaining one while showing the other.

By coming into the streets with us, you will learn that not everything you think you "know" is true. The stereotypes, the myths, the lies we've been told about other people are often wrong when we get face to face with someone and truly get to know them. Love breaks down these barriers, it breaks through walls, and it reaches to the heart. Love changes lives, empowers connection, and builds community.

We are so blessed to have such incredible people come alongside us, get to know our friends, and show them love in the process. It truly is such a beautiful experience.
