Snapshots From the Street

Moments of grace from this month.

Extra pizzas, extra smiles

Thank you Impact 100 Gulf Coast for sending us off tonight with the extra pizzas! Because of your generosity, we were able to spontaneously serve 12 friends in the streets tonight with abundance!

We love the nights we get to drive around, find friends, and fill their bellies unannounced and unexpectedly!

The extra smiles, extra hugs, and extra gratitude make it worth every second!

Simple acts of service

Simple acts of service. From an empty parking lot to full set up.

Every Thursday our friends on the street are entrusted and empowered to serve. JL leaves the trailer there after the morning serving. We have a friend on the street that now leads a team of other friends in setting everything up for the evening service. Without fail, it is done every week!

Its simple. Yet glorious!

Its the little things. Truly.

Holidays don’t stop hunger

Hunger doesn't take holidays so neither do we. Stocked and ready to serve our friends in the street tonight with our incredible team!

Happy Memorial Day from the Salty Farm Fam! We serve many veterans and are thankful for their service and for the service of all men and women. Your sacrifice gives us the opportunity to serve others week in and week out. Thank you. Truly.

A Story From This Month
A powerful glimpse of transformation: a friend who once came to be served is now serving others, walking in sobriety and taking step after step toward a new life.

When a friend on the street goes from being served on one side of the table to serving from the other side...

Their hearts to give, serve, and love on each other blow me away time and time again.

So proud of our friend for working hard to get off the streets, staying sober, and taking the next right step to make sure homelessness was a short chapter and didn't become an identity he carried around!

We are honored that the impact the ministry had on him drives him to turn around and impact others with his love and hope he can give them to follow in his foot steps!
