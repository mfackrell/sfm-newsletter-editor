The Heart of Salty Farm Ministries

Restoring dignity, meeting needs, and sharing the love of Jesus with the forgotten places of Bay County.

“The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.”

Testimony

Across Bay County, pockets of deep need often go unseen—people living without showers, clean clothes, or even a place to feel human again. Salty Farm Ministries stepped into these very places with one mission: restore dignity, hope, and purpose through the love of Jesus.

Through mobile showers, clothing distribution, hygiene care, haircuts, and warm meals, volunteers meet people right where they are. Every week, teams travel to multiple sites, offering practical help but also genuine relationship. Many who felt abandoned discover, maybe for the first time, that God still sees them.

As testimonies are shared and prayers offered, hearts soften. What begins with soap, a shirt, or a sandwich often becomes an encounter with the gospel. Lives shift. Hope returns. Community forms. And the streets of Bay County slowly begin to look a little more like the Kingdom of God.

"Dignity is often restored long before a life is changed—and that opens the door for Jesus to heal what’s been broken." — Salty Farm Ministries
