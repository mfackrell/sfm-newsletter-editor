A Movement of Compassion

Restoring Dignity and Hope
One Street at a Time

How God is moving through Salty Farm Ministries to meet needs, lift the broken, and reveal Jesus to the hurting of Bay County, Florida.

Key Scripture

“The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.”

Psalm 34:18 (NIV)

Testimony

On the streets of Bay County, Florida, where many feel overlooked and unseen, Salty Farm Ministries shows up week after week as a living reminder that Jesus still seeks out the lost, the hurting, and the forgotten. Their ministry began with a simple desire: meet people right where they are and restore dignity in ways that feel practical, human, and full of love.

With their mobile “one‑stop” outreach, they provide hot showers, clean clothes, hygiene kits, food, haircuts, and compassionate conversation. What many see as small acts become life-changing moments for those who haven't felt valued in a long time. For people battling addiction, homelessness, or deep personal brokenness, a warm shower or a fresh haircut becomes a step toward believing that they matter again.

Every location, every interaction, every shared testimony becomes a living sermon of God’s grace. And as Jesus is shared openly—through conversations, prayer, and consistent presence—hearts begin to soften. People who once avoided eye contact now ask for prayer. Those who felt forgotten begin to hope again. This isn’t just a mission—it’s a movement the Holy Spirit is breathing on, calling people back to life.

"When you show people the love of Jesus through dignity, compassion, and consistency—hearts open, and hope rises." — Salty Farm Ministries

Reflection
