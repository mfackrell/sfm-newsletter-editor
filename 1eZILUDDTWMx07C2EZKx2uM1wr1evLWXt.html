When God Moves Through a Simple
Yes

Key Scripture

“Whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me.”

Matthew 25:40 NIV

When a sudden cold snap chilled Florida, the county and Rebuild Bay opened the A.D. Harris Learning Village as a temporary warming shelter. With almost no notice, Salty Farm Ministries said yes—first serving their normal Monday street outreach, then continuing late into the night caring for the unhoused in crisis.

Testimony

The shelter quickly filled with men and women desperate for warmth, dignity, and hope. Volunteers from Salty Farm stepped in, offering showers, clean clothing, prayer, and presence. That first night alone saw nearly 20 showers and over 30 people clothed—many of them individuals the ministry had never met before.

By Friday, the team returned and broke another record: 29 showers in a single night. Every towel handed out, every conversation shared, and every prayer offered became a moment where Jesus touched someone’s life through simple obedience.

It wasn’t about numbers. It wasn’t about tasks. It was about relationships—real, human connections that reflect the heart of Christ. This is the Gospel lived out in cold hallways, in late-night service, and in whispered prayers beside weary souls.

What God Revealed

  • God moves powerfully through last‑minute obedience.
  • Ministry is about relationship, not transaction.
  • The unhoused are seen, known, and loved by Jesus.
  • Every volunteer’s yes becomes part of the Great Commission.
  • God creates divine appointments when His people show up.

“Ministry is not a transaction—it's a relationship that carries the heart of Jesus.”

Reflection

Maybe you feel like you have little to offer. Maybe you think your yes is too small. But God transforms small yeses into eternal impact. The same Jesus who meets the unhoused in a warming shelter meets you in your own place of need—and He invites you to join Him in loving others back to life.

Call to Action

Today, offer God your yes. Pray for opportunities. Serve with compassion. Volunteer, give, or partner with us at SaltyFarmMinistries.org. Every act of love spreads the name of Jesus to those who need Him most.

Closing Prayer

“Jesus, use my yes. Open my heart to the hurting and make me a vessel of Your love. Amen.”
