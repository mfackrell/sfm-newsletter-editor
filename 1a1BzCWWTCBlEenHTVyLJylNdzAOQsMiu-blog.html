Salty Talk Devotional

When Obedience Feels Invisible
God Is Still Moving

JL and Michelle share the unseen stories proving that every act of love matters, even when no fruit appears at first.

Key Scripture

“Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up.”

Galatians 6:9 (NIV)

Testimony

JL and Michelle have learned that some of God’s greatest works happen in moments nobody sees. Their ministry is built on simple, consistent obedience—showing up, loving people, and trusting God with the outcome. Over time, they discovered that these “small” acts were actually the seeds of life-changing transformation.

They shared stories of God’s fingerprints showing up in unexpected places: propane running out mid-shower, only for God to use the inconvenience to align hearts; a concert delayed so the Holy Spirit could move powerfully; spontaneous baptisms that completely changed lives; and Clay—once hardened and hopeless—now walking transformed by Jesus.

Again and again, they saw the same truth: it’s not our job to fix or save people. Our role is obedience. God’s role is everything else. And when we show up faithfully, He writes stories no human could author.

"Our job isn’t to fix people—our job is to love them and trust God with the rest." — Salty Farm Ministries

What God Revealed

Obedience Matters Even When Unseen

God moves behind the scenes long before fruit appears.

We Aren’t the Savior—Jesus Is

We simply show love; He transforms hearts.

Small Acts Become Eternal Seeds

A conversation, a prayer, a warm shower—God uses it all.

Every Person Is Worth Showing Up For

Reflection

Maybe you’ve been loving someone who hasn’t changed yet. Maybe you’ve been praying and seeing nothing happen. Take heart—God sees what you can’t. He is writing a story far bigger than this moment. Keep showing up. Keep loving. Keep trusting. Jesus is alive, and He is still in the business of resurrection.

Call to Action

Pray today for renewed strength to love boldly. Ask Jesus to break chains, restore hearts, and use your obedience in ways you may never see. And if you feel led, partner with Salty Farm Ministries—serve, volunteer, or give $15/month to provide hot showers and dignity to those who need hope.
