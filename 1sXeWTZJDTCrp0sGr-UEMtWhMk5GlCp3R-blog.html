Healing God Stories
Miracles in the Streets

Key Scripture

“We live by faith, not by sight.”

2 Corinthians 5:7 (NIV)

In this episode of Salty Talk, the faithfulness of God is woven through stories of healing, provision, and obedience. From a supernatural moment of restored sight to unexpected answers to prayer, the testimony reminds us that God still moves in power and that He meets people right where they are—whether in a church pew, on the street, or in the quiet of their prayers.

Testimony

One of the most powerful stories shared was about a man at Golf View UMC who had been struggling with blindness. After being prayed for, he opened his eyes and began to see—stunned, shaking, and overwhelmed. It was a moment described as nothing short of a Damascus encounter with Jesus. His life shifted instantly, not only because his sight was restored, but because he knew God had touched him personally.

But the episode didn’t stop with a single miracle. It also highlighted the journey of healing—how some works of God happen instantly while others unfold slowly, step by step. The message was clear: God is the Healer, and He meets each person in the way they need Him most.

Another story revealed God's provision in ministry. A growing need to provide first-aid care for the homeless seemed impossible to meet—until a retired nurse, led by dreams and stirred by prayer, reached out and offered her time and compassion. What looked like coincidence was unmistakably God’s orchestration. And now, as the ministry awaits a February 18 grant decision for a heavy-duty pickup to tow the mobile shower trailer, the call to prayer continues. Every step forward is another opportunity to watch God provide.

What God Revealed

  • God still performs miracles, both instant and gradual.
  • Healing is often a journey that shapes faith along the way.
  • Obedience opens doors for God to use ordinary people in extraordinary ways.
  • God provides the right people at the right time for His work.
  • The church is called to serve beyond its walls, every day, everywhere.

“God meets us where we are, but He never leaves us where He found us.”

Reflection

Maybe you find yourself hoping for a miracle, or maybe you’re walking through a slow, painful process that feels endless. Take heart—Jesus is not done with you. He sees your wounds, your doubts, your exhaustion, and He draws near. Whether your breakthrough comes suddenly or gradually, His faithfulness remains the same. He is the God who heals, restores, and provides. Lean into Him. Ask boldly. Trust deeply. And don’t walk the journey alone.

Call to Action

Today, choose faith over fear. Ask God where He is calling you to serve outside your comfort zone. Pray for those in need, especially our homeless neighbors. And please join us in praying for the February 18 grant decision—believing with us that God will provide the truck needed to move the shower trailer and expand this ministry. Say yes to God’s leading, and watch Him move.

Closing Prayer

“Jesus, open our eyes to Your power and love. Heal our hearts, strengthen our faith, and guide our steps. Make us bold to serve and quick to obey. Let Your miracles—seen and unseen—continue to unfold in our lives. Amen.”
