Snapshots From the Street

Moments of grace from this month.

From being served to serving

When a friend on the street goes from being served on one side of the table to serving from the other side... 😋

Their hearts to give, serve, and love on each other blow me away time and time again.

So proud of our friend for working hard to get off the streets, staying sober, and taking the next right step to make sure homelessness was a short chapter and didn't become an identity he carried around!

We are honored that the impact the ministry had on him drives him to turn around and impact others with his love and hope he can give them to follow in his foot steps! 🙌

Already set up when we arrived

This is what the ones we serve do for us. I just pulled in and this is all set up by our friends.

Thats amazing. Their hearts to serve are mind blowing. We could learn so much from them.

Worship with our brothers and sisters

Salty showed up at Engage Victory with BeGenerous, Inc. 🙌 always love worshipping with our brothers and sisters! ❤️

A Story From This Month
A serve night where the parking lot felt like holy ground—connection, conversations, and next steps toward hope unfolding in real time.

Last night was something truly special. The flow of the Lord, His Spirit, and His love was palpable.

The parking lot was full of lives longing for connection. The sandwich, the shower is just a means for relationship.

The beauty of it all happens in the conversations, the hugs, the celebration of successes, the mourning losses, the simply doing life together.

Resources came to help for one reason... reach people and meet needs. Seeds were planted, treatment sought out, next right steps obtained. 🙌

There simply isn't enough words that adequately describes what happens in the parking lot. All I can say it is simply beautiful and truly an honor to get to witness.

This is Holy Ground because HE IS IN IT, HE IS PRESENT. Lives are changing every week, no matter what side of the table you find yourself on. God is good! ❤️
