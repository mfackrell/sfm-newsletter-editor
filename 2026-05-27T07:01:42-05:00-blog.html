God Sees the Hidden Work

Even in the Whirlwind,
God Provides

A testimony of divine favor, unexpected encouragement, and the God who notices what others overlook.

Key Scripture

“Your Father who sees what is done in secret will reward you.”

Matthew 6:6 (NIV)

Testimony

This week was a whirlwind for JL and Michelle as they moved their fifth‑wheel home, unsure how everything would work out. Yet from free parking for their plants and trailers to a surprise comped meal by a supporter, God kept reminding them that He sees their needs and hears their prayers. Every detail whispered His care.

In the midst of financial questions surrounding the ministry, they received a word: “More is coming.” Soon after, they partnered with Abel Ministries during PCB Bike Week, joining a faith‑based rehab and trade program committed to reaching the broken through relationship instead of judgment. Their outreach team watched God move as hardened homeless men—often resistant—softened when met with simple love, food, and presence. Some encounters happened through planned routine, others from spontaneous pop‑ins that turned into holy moments.

But one of the deepest parts of the week was Michelle’s private battle with feeling unseen. She asked God to confirm whether He really noticed her behind‑the‑scenes work. The very next day during worship, she was called out publicly and given a powerful prophetic word: she carries an anointing, a fire, and God is preparing to expand her influence. She was told that in about six months, she would step into a breakthrough she had been praying for. God saw her. God answered.

"God doesn’t overlook the hidden places—He rewards the faith nobody else sees." — Salty Farm Ministries

What God Revealed

God sees the unnoticed work.

Hidden obedience is never hidden from Him.

Provision comes at the perfect moment.

Even free parking and a surprise meal can be divine reminders.

Love opens doors Scripture alone cannot.

Relationship softens the hardest hearts.

Breakthrough is often already scheduled by God.

He prepares victory long before we feel it.

Reflection

If you’ve ever felt unseen, overlooked, or unsure whether God notices the quiet battles you fight—take heart. He sees every sacrifice, every prayer prayed in the dark, every act of love that no one else applauds. The same God who met JL and Michelle in the whirlwind is ready to meet you in yours. Jesus restores, heals, and brings breakthrough in His timing and His power.
