Testimony of God’s Faithfulness

When God Sees You
In the Chaos

A whirlwind week, unexpected favor, and a prophetic reminder that God never overlooks His servants.

Key Scripture

“The eyes of the Lord are on the righteous, and his ears are attentive to their cry.”

Psalm 34:15 (NIV)

Testimony

JL and Michelle stepped into their week already exhausted. Ministry had been nonstop, and moving their fifth-wheel trailer felt like one more impossible task. Yet as they navigated the chaos, God kept dropping signs of favor—free parking for the trailer, unexpected help, and even a meal paid for without them asking. Each provision whispered the same message: “I’m with you. I go before you.”

Their week included partnering with Abel Ministries during Panama City Beach bike week. Abel Ministries, a faith-based rehab and trades program, invited them to join a Q&A session and community outreach. Together they hit the streets and parks—offering prayer, conversation, and simple presence. What surprised them wasn’t the logistics or the crowds but how hearts softened almost instantly when they simply showed up with love. They saw the power of both structured ministry and holy interruptions. God moved in scheduled moments and in unplanned encounters.

But underneath the obedience, Michelle carried a quiet ache. She had been asking the Lord, “Do You see me? Do You really notice what I’m pouring out?” The weight of unseen service had been heavy. Then—out of nowhere—she was publicly called out and prophesied over. The word confirmed everything she had been wrestling with: she was a fire, a mouthpiece, a trailblazer for the Kingdom. JL was spoken over too—anointed, visionary, chosen for leadership. They were told the obstacles before them were already being moved and that within six months they would step into the breakthrough they had been praying for.

That prophetic moment reignited their spirits. It reminded them that the God who sees every hidden sacrifice is also the God who rewards openly. With renewed courage, they’re pressing forward—inviting others to serve with them, asking for prayer over a $100k grant decision on May 12 for their food truck ministry, and believing the best is still ahead.

"God doesn’t just see what you’re doing—He sees you. And He is moving every obstacle out of your way." — Salty Farm Ministries

What God Revealed

God’s favor shows up in the ordinary

Even small blessings are signs that He goes before His people.

Showing up with love can change a heart faster than any sermon.

God sees the unseen sacrifice

He responds to quiet faithfulness with public affirmation.

Breakthrough is already in motion

What looks delayed is often being built behind the scenes.

Reflection

If you’ve ever wondered whether God sees your struggle, your service, or your silent exhaustion—take heart. The same God who met JL and Michelle in their fatigue wants to remind you that He sees you. He has not overlooked your obedience. He cherishes every “yes,” even the ones whispered through tears. Jesus draws near to the weary and lifts up the broken. Let Him breathe fresh courage into you today.

Call to Action

Pray for Salty Farm Ministries as they seek God’s provision, especially for the May 12 decision on the $100k food truck grant. Ask the Lord how you can serve, partner, or support the work He’s doing through this ministry. Step out in faith—your breakthrough may be closer than you think.
