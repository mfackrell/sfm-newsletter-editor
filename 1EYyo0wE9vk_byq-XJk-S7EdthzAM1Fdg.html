A Story From This Month
A “we do it different” day in the streets turned into a God-orchestrated rescue—connecting a man with serious needs to the exact help he needed, right on time.

We do it different.

We go to the streets. We meet them where they are. We don't expect them to have it all together before we provide services. We do whatever it takes to get them where they need to be. We collaborate with other like-minded organizations. We are kingdom focused.

Our Vice President, Ray Sprague, didn't give up on this man. He reached out to JL and I yesterday, defeated and unsure what to do. Nowhere in our town would provide services. So, I reached out to a resource out of our area, Stephanie at Freedom House -Transitional Housing who is well connected and also does whatever it takes! With her help, Ray got him to Waterfront Rescue Mission which is exactly where he needed to be! They are going to help get him connected to everything he needs to get back on his feet!

Here's where the story gets even crazier...the intake chaplain is Palmer Davis who happens to be the very first resource we connected with in PCB when we got here (he used to be with Haven House)! He is the one that connected us with many other resources in the beginning.

It just amazes me how God connects people long before we know what that connection will produce. In this case, it got a man with medical needs, no physical ability to work, but no disability, no insurance, and no home into a place that will help him acquire the assistance he needs to get back on his feet and not just discharging him to the streets with a "good luck and figure it out."

When Palmer was doing his intake he told this gentleman,

"You just relax, we are going to take care of you. You aren't going back out on the streets."

Could you imagine the breath he took in that moment. The way his shoulders lowered just a little in knowing he was safe again.

God is ALWAYS working even when we don't see it. Today, we are thankful for everyone that had a hand in getting this man off the streets and gave him some hope again! 🙌

Volunteer Spotlight

This is the testimony of one of our volunteers and leaders, Nichole Rambaran...

📢📢📢

"2015. Lost. Broken. A hot mess. 20 years addicted to meth/xanex/weed/needles/WHATEVER I COULD OBTAIN. Complex ptsd, agoraphobia, behavioral personality disorder, anxiety, depression. Serious mental instability. Starving for food, life, love, attention. Raising 2 little girls alone, pregnant with a 3rd. I did know how I would buy Christmas this year. This was the year Jesus began to really speak to me. And I was too addicted/broken/LOST to get to Him.

It would take 2 years from this point before I would begin my journey to full surrender. Even in the darkness. The high. The witchcraft. The sexual Addiction. He was speaking to me, wooing me. When I hit the lowest of low, He was waiting. And He got me.

Today.... I belong to Him. Only Him. Sober for 4 years. Thriving. My children are thriving. I am loved/living/validated by Christ. I am a new creation.

When I look at this broken woman, and I am reminded that I was broken as a little girl. And this woman was her. Today, I am a grown woman, and a daughter of God. Not a broken little girl.

The enemy has lost his grip on me. I am Blood Bought. Redeemed. Restored. Healed. Whole. Forgiven. Loved. Called. Honored.

My journey through that hell was so that I could grab others by the hand and pull them out of it, and help them to the foot of the Cross. Where they can find freedom.

After many years of losing to the devil, I realize: the devil got his butt kicked. And my Jesus don't play about me."

🙌🙌🙌🙌🙌🙌

Today, she is a warrior in the streets! Rescuing others as she leads them to the cross. Ushering the broken to freedom.

NEVER feel like God can't use you! The very story of your life could be the very testimony someone needs to hear to pull them from the pit! Never underestimate what God can do in your life and how He can use you afterwards!

If you are struggling with similar battles that she found freedom from, reach out to her! I promise you will not find a better battle partner!

That is what I LOVE about our Salty Farm Fam! There is ZERO judgement, no condemnation. Simply others who have walked through messes, brokenness, addictions, and much more but have simply found our way to Jesus. We don't have it all together. But we definitely don't walk through it alone. Need a community that becomes family? Join us! ❤️
