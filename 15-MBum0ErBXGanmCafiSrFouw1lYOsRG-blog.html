The Hidden Ways God Is Moving
When We Simply Show Up

A reminder that God moves in unseen places, through small acts of obedience, and in the hearts we least expect.

Key Scripture

“Let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven.”

Matthew 5:16 (NIV)

Testimony

In a recent conversation at Salty Farm Ministries, JL and Michelle shared how God continues to move in quiet, often unseen ways. Their hearts burn for the overlooked and the broken—those who rarely make it into a church building but desperately need the love of Christ. Through simple obedience and the willingness to show up, they have watched countless “God stories” unfold.

They talked about moments that seemed insignificant at the time, like a day when the portable shower trailer wasn’t producing hot water. Embarrassed, they apologized to a man expecting a warm shower. But instead of frustration, he smiled and said, “You showed up. That’s what matters.” God used even the imperfect moments to reveal His heart.

They shared how sometimes the fruit comes later—a concert outreach that seemed to have little impact at the moment, only for someone months later to say that night changed their life. A baptism that marked the turning of a soul from darkness to light. And even Clay, a man once hardened and distant, who is now asking for a Bible and seeking the truth of God for himself.

Through every story, one truth echoed deeply: it is not our job to fix people or save them. Our calling is to love, serve, disciple, and trust that God is working beneath the surface, often in ways we cannot see. Souls and eternity are in the balance, and God invites each believer to step into the mission field of daily life—carrying the hope of Jesus wherever brokenness is found.

"We aren’t called to fix people. We’re called to love them, show up for them, and let God do the saving." — Salty Farm Ministries

What God Revealed

God Works in the Unseen

He moves in quiet moments where no one is watching and no applause is given.

Obedience Matters More Than Perfection

God honors simple faithfulness—even cold showers and messy moments.

We Are Called to Love, Not Fix

Jesus heals hearts. Our role is to shine His light and serve with compassion.

Lives Are Being Changed

From baptisms to seekers like Clay, God is softening hearts in powerful ways.

Reflection

Maybe you’ve believed the lie that what you do doesn’t matter. Maybe you feel unseen, unsure, or too broken to make a difference. But God delights in using ordinary people to carry extraordinary hope. You do not need to have the perfect words or the perfect life—just a willing heart. Someone is waiting for the love of Jesus inside you.

Call to Action

Pray for courage to show up where God places you. Lean into community. Let Jesus heal what’s broken in you so you can help bring healing to others. And if you feel led, partner with Salty Farm Ministries—volunteer, pray, or support the mission. $15 a month helps provide showers, outreach, and the message of Jesus to those who need it most.
